“School’s Out” Screenshot Competition Concludes! ✈️

Winning photo:

📊 On June 29th, at around 6 PM EST, the voting for winner of the “School’s Out” screenshot competition started. This voting consisted of 5 screenshots chosen by ATC24 staff, which the community could vote on to win.

🏆 On June 30th, the user “Antxley” was announced as the winner of the competition in the ATC24 server.

💸 This competition was the first in ATC24 history to have ATC24 premium as a reward. Hence, Antxley was the first community member to win ATC24 premium from an ATC24 contest.
